# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-08

We store minimal data locally to power core features (habits, reminders, preferences).
Diagnostics and crash reports are opt-in. You can change your choice anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security.
You can delete all locally stored data from Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete all data.
No personal data is shared with third parties without your consent.

For questions, contact: support@example.com
